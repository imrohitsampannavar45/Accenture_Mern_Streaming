  • Responsiveness - Compatible / Adaptable to all the devices

Bootstrap Grid System

  • Grid - Layout - Consist of rows and columns
  • Each Row can have upto 12 columns

  • Bootstrap Include 4 Different Classes
  • Large Devices - lg - 1200px - Laptops and Desktops
  • Medium Devices - md- 992px - Laptops
  • Small Devices - sm- 768px - Tablets
  • Extra Small Devices - xs - less than 768px - Mobile Phone
